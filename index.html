Can tastes cancel each other out?

Taste interactions can be enhancing or suppressing. Typically, if you have a sweet food, adding salt or acid will suppress the sweetness (think salt with sweet caramel or acidic raspberries with sweet white chocolate). Salty and sweet both suppress bitterness (for example, salty chips with bitter beer). 17 Aug 2023
